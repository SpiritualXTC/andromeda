Shaders:
--------
Geometry Shader for Rendering Vertex Normals
 - Render the geometry as points.
 - Geometry shader processes the points into lines representing the normal

Geometry Shader for Rendering Face Normals
 - Render the geometry as triangles.
 - Geometry shader averages the 3 vertices to find the face center. 
 - From the center draw a normal from the average of the normals
